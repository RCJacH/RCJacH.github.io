An Elaborated Summary of the Technique of Gain-Staging - 1. What and Why

April 7, 2019 · 1128 words
Music Production
Music Production · REAPER · Recording · Mixing

What is Gain-Staging Gain-staging is essentially the practice of managing all static volume settings with a fixed reference point. Static volume setting is decibels of gain added or subtracted to a signal at one place in the signal flow, which remains unchanged through out the time span of the project playback. The goal of gain-staging is to ensure that any pair of input and output signals enclosing a signal processor chain match in average volume.
